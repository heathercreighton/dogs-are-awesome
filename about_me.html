Meet Heather!

As a Detroit native, Heather has always been interested in engineering and technology. She attended Davis Aerospace High School and studied airplane instrumentation. She continued her education at The University of Michigan in their Electrical Engineering and Computer Science program. This is where she was first introduced to the world of coding via Fortran and Matlab (Formula Translation)

Upon graduation, Heather worked in the Automotive industry designing wiring harnesses for the Big 3 Automotive companies and several foreign car companines. It would be 3 years before Heather entered a career in programming as an SAP/Abap Consultant, specializing in data migration/conversion, batch data processing and electronic data interchange, and user reports.

Today, Heather is working on expanding her skillset with training in HTML, CSS, PHP, Ruby on Rails, and Javascript.
